To the end

A journey not eastwards

Sol loaded his sling, stood up from the cover of the bushes and swung his weapon. Once, twice, thrice, and then let the missile loose. The stone flew whistling through the air, striking one of the slavers right in the back of her helmet. The slaver tottered and almost fell but quickly recovered her posture. Sol reached for his weapong again, but his second hasted stone hit the shield of his target harmlessly. There would be not time for a third try.
